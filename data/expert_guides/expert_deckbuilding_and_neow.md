# Deckbuilding mindset & Neow (expert)

- **Avoid locking an “archetype” on floor 1.** You add cards one pick at a time; build a deck that can survive early elites, then lean into synergies that actually appear.
- **Prefer organic synergies** between cards you already hold over forcing a theme (e.g. “this run must be Claw”).
- **Card roles (community framing):** front-loaded damage, AoE/crowd control, draw/cycle, combo pieces vs combo engines (payoffs that need setup).
- **Neow’s Lament** (“next **3 combats**, enemies have **1 HP**”): wiki states this applies to **normal, elite, and boss** fights—so it can enable a **planned early elite** if the **map** supports it. **Preview the map** before locking the blessing. (See [Neow's Lament](https://slay-the-spire.fandom.com/wiki/Neow%27s_Lament).)
- **Random rare card** is high variance—great bombs exist, but dead draws can ruin Act 1.
- **Swap starter for boss relic** can redefine the run; some characters depend on their starter relic more than others—weight risk vs upside.
