# Act 3, elites, bosses & Heart (expert)

- **Hall fights are punishing**—exploders on timers, spikers, Darklings revive rules, Transient clock, etc. **Fix deck holes** with shops/rest/events before gambling.
- **Elites:** **Reptomancer**—kill **daggers** before they detonate; AoE mvp. **Nemesis**—use **Intangible turns** to set up, strike on vulnerable turns. **Stone Head (Stone calendar boss)**—**Slow** rewards **play order** (setup then big finish).
- **Awakened One:** clear **cultists** first; **delay non-essential Powers** while passive gains Str from Powers; unload after phase change.
- **Donu & Deca:** Fandom **tips**: if you **cannot out-block** Donu’s **Circle of Power** scaling, **focus Donu first**; if **Deca’s Dazes** will choke a **low-draw** deck, **focus Deca first**. Malaise / Corpse Explosion lines exist—watch **Artifact** charges.
- **Time Eater:** wiki: **12 cards played** ends your turn early and it gains **Strength** (counter can carry over). Spread value across turns; avoid ending a turn at **10–11** plays without a plan; handle the **heal to half** threshold if you can’t burst through.
- **Act 4 / Heart (high level):** need **consistent block + scaling damage**; **Beat of Death** (wiki: chip damage when you **play a card**—intensity varies with ascension / buffs); the Heart also uses a **per-turn damage cap** so “infinite damage” combos don’t trivialise the fight—plan **sustained** offense/defense. **Multi-hit** attacks are mitigated well by **Strength reduction** (e.g. Dark Shackles, Piercing Wail). Bring **strong potions** when the map allows. **Confirm numbers in-game.**
