# Act 1 gear checks (expert)

- **Elites are largely damage races.** Prioritize **damage first**, then shore up defense; **upgrades** matter—upgrade your highest-impact commons early (Vulnerable on Bash is often huge for Ironclad).
- **Gremlin Nob:** punishes playing Skills (gains Strength). Favor **Attacks** and burst; Weak helps Silent; plan orb/burst for Defect.
- **Lagavulin:** starts asleep—use turns to set up **Powers** and draw; after wake, kill before **-Str/-Dex** debuffs snowball.
- **Sentinels:** kill one Sentinel quickly; **AoE** (Cleave, Dagger Spray, etc.) is strong. Track turn pattern (middle then outers).
- **Slime Boss:** race **pre-split** HP; **AoE** cleans split phase; Evolve-style answers help vs Slimed.
- **Hexaghost:** you want **scaling** (Strength, vulnerable synergy, repeatable damage). **Divider** (wiki-documented): damage scales with **current HP** at the time of the attack—Fandom gives *(H+1)×6* total for the multi-hit with *H = ⌊current HP / 12⌋* (same formula appears on [wiki.gg Hexaghost](https://slaythespire.wiki.gg/wiki/Hexaghost)). **Entering** the boss fight at a lower HP can reduce that attack; always **read intent** in-game after patches.
- **Guardian:** alternating modes—**thorns while defensive**; time damage for safe windows, keep block up in bad phases.
