# Act 2 scaling & bosses (expert)

- **You need scaling** if you only have hallway damage—Strength engines, poison/catalyst lines, X-cost payoffs, relic-driven synergies.
- **Hallway threats are varied:** Byrds need multi-hit to ground; plant punishes many small hits; shelled parasite punishes stalls; triple cultist wants fast AoE or big hits.
- **Book of Stabbing:** **end fast**; Wounds choke the deck; multi-hit ramps—Weak / Str reduction help.
- **Gremlin Leader:** either **burn the boss** and ignore adds, or **clear adds** if you have AoE—don’t let buff cycles run away.
- **Slaver trio:** **kill the backliner (net)** first to avoid losing key turns to Entangle, then clean wounds.
- **Champ:** plan around **50% transition**—don’t cross until ready; phase 2 hits much harder (often kill race or strong block engines).
- **Collector:** debuffs + minion pressure—**don’t stall**; AoE and burst are premium; sometimes leave one weak minion to block resummon cadence.
- **Bronze Automaton:** plan for **Hyper Beam** (wiki: large telegraphed hit). Either **race** before it lands or **mitigate** with a concrete block/intangible plan—**use in-game intent**, not a memorized turn count. Killing an orb can **return a stolen card**.
