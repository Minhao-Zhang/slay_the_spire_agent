# Watcher reward & build notes

- **Stances**: plan exits—don’t enter wrath without a kill, block, or stance change.
- **Scrying**: use to set up wrath turns and avoid drawing dead on critical turns.
- **Calm / divinity**: treat energy spikes as burst windows; don’t waste them on low-impact plays.
- Rewards: favor pieces that make stance transitions safe (talk to the hand, wallop, etc.).
