# Ironclad reward & build notes

- **Strength / exhaust**: leverage strength payoffs when you can convert them into fast kills.
- **Block**: Barricade-like lines want consistent block engines; don’t dilute with low-tempo attacks.
- **Corruption / dead branch**: high variance—take when support exists (draw, energy, exhaust synergy).
- Rewards: prioritize cards that solve Act elites (Nob/Lagavulin checks) early.
