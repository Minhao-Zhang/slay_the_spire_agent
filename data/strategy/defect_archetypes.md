# Defect reward & build notes

- **Orbs**: focus on channels/focus/evoke sequencing; don’t float orbs with no plan.
- **Claw**: needs thin deck or heavy draw—verify you can play multiple cheap cards per turn.
- **Frost / block**: strong for long fights; pair with scaling damage to avoid timer losses.
- Prioritize cards that stabilize Act 1 elites while preserving a late-game win condition.
