# Elite combat heuristics

- Treat elites as gear checks: you need a plan for their first 2–3 turns, not only “average” turns.
- Potions are fair currency—using a strong potion to save 30+ HP is often +1 future elite.
- Respect intent: if a big hit is coming, blocking it beats playing more damage *this* turn if you die next.
