# Act 1 pacing

- Front-load damage and front-load block early; enemies hit hard relative to starter defenses.
- Take cards that solve **next** elite (Gremlin Nob, Lagavulin, Slavers) even if they are narrow.
- Early pathing: extra elites are valuable if you have potions/HP to convert; avoid double-elite routes on low HP without answers.
- Shops: early removals are premium if you can afford them without skipping a needed common.
