# Boss fight heuristics

- Identify the **real clock** (boss buffs, multi-hit, strength scaling) and spend resources there.
- Do not over-kill minions if the boss phase is the loss condition (e.g., timer/transform mechanics).
- Front-load mitigation on telegraphed big turns; chip damage is fine if you survive the spike.
- Powers and setup: play them when you can afford the tempo hit, not blindly on turn 1.
