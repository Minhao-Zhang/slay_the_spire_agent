# Curated strategy reference (community-aligned)

This file distills widely repeated guidance from public Slay the Spire community resources
(wikis, tier lists, and long-form guides). It is **not** verbatim text from any single source;
it is a short synthesis for LLM context. For primary references, see the
[Slay the Spire Wiki](https://slay-the-spire.fandom.com/wiki/Slay_the_Spire_Wiki) and
class-specific guides maintained by the community.

## Pathing and pacing

- Elites and bosses are the main checks; normal fights are partly about setup and resources.
- Weigh path risk using current HP, potions, and whether the deck has answered known upcoming threats.
- Act transitions and boss floors are natural points to pivot deck direction.

## Deck building

- Thin the deck of starter chaff and dead draws when removal is affordable; density beats raw card count.
- Prioritize cards that solve the next hard fight, not only abstract “value.”
- Draw and energy are multipliers; scaling without delivery is fragile.

## Combat heuristics

- Respect enemy intent: kill, block, or debuff based on what actually prevents damage next turn.
- Front-load block or kill when multi-attacks or big hits are telegraphed.
- Do not spend all resources answering minions if the boss timer is the real loss condition.

## Resources

- Potions are spike answers; prefer spending them on elites and bosses when HP is tight.
- Gold timing: shops are stronger when the card pool is wider and gold has accumulated.
