# Act 2 pacing

- Enemies scale faster than Act 1; AoE and front-loaded block become more important.
- Prioritize solving **multi-enemy** fights: target order matters (disruptors, big hitters, then cleanup).
- Elites are run-defining—check potion + HP before committing to extra elites.
- Event risk spikes; read options carefully when HP is low or deck is thin on block.
