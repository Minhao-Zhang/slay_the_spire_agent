# General principles

Distilled community guidance for StS agents (not verbatim from any single source).

- **Threat order**: solve the next loss condition first—often elites/bosses, not every hallway.
- **Deck density**: fewer dead draws beats higher card count; removals are often tempo-positive.
- **Scaling needs delivery**: draw and energy multiply good cards; raw scaling with no draw is fragile.
- **Intent-driven turns**: block, kill, or debuff based on what actually prevents damage *next* enemy turn.
- **Resources**: spend potions when they prevent an elite/boss spike; hoarding through Act 2 often costs HP.
