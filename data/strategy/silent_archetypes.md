# Silent reward & build notes

- **Poison / scaling**: needs time and defense; weak and block are multipliers.
- **Shivs**: need accuracy, draw, or damage scaling—avoid thin shiv packages without enablers.
- **Discard**: pay attention to energy and hand-size churn; don’t take discard payoffs without outlets.
- Early Act 1: front-loaded damage and efficient block often beat slow poison starts.
