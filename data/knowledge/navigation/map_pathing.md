# Map pathing and elite snowball

- **Risk budget**: choose paths using HP, potions, and whether the deck answers **upcoming elites**.
- **Flexible routing** beats a rigid “elite every node” plan if one bad fight would end the run—detour for **rest/shop** when behind curve; snowball elites only when answers are real.
- Elites and bosses are main checks; hallways are fuel and setup. **Extra elites** snowball relics and gold when you can convert HP safely—bail if you lack answers.
- Act transitions are pivots—re-path if the deck gained block, scaling, or new weaknesses.
- Prefer forks that keep options open; dead-end risky lines punish bad reads.
- **Elite → campfire** lets you spend HP for a relic then recover; **elite → punishing hallway** stacks risk without a safety node.
- Preview the map before blessings (e.g. Neow options) that assume a specific fight order.
