# Sources & verification (not retrieved into prompts)

Knowledge corpus files are short paraphrases. Below: credible tiers and where key claims were cross-checked (February 2026).

## Source tiers

1. Slay the Spire Wiki (Fandom) — Primary verifier for mechanics, attacks, buffs, boss tips.
2. Slay the Spire Wiki (wiki.gg) — Second read on boss pages.
3. slaythespire.gg guides — Community opinion; heuristics not raw numbers.
4. Reddit / Steam — Tertiary consensus when aligned with wiki.

Official game text + in-game intents are ground truth after patches.

## Reference vs knowledge (powers)

- `data/reference/powers.json` holds **per-power effect strings** used in prompts (`effect=` on powers, **BUFF GLOSSARY**). That is the right place for “what does Vulnerable do?” style facts.
- `data/knowledge/fundamentals/game_mechanics.md` should explain **how systems combine** (turn flow, hand cap, card keywords vs combat powers, float damage, orb Focus rules) without copying those effect lines.

## Topic notes

- Neow's Lament: next 3 combats enemies at 1 HP — applies to normal, elite, boss; preview map before blessing.
- Gremlin Nob: Skills trigger Rage/Strength — favor Attacks and burst.
- Hexaghost Divider: damage scales with current HP at attack time — read in-game intent.
- Time Eater: 12 cards played ends player turn early; gains Strength.
- Book of Stabbing: Wounds on unblocked hits — end fast.
- Awakened One: gains Strength from Powers in phase 1 — delay non-essential Powers.
- Donu & Deca: focus Donu if Circle of Power out-scales block; focus Deca if Dazes choke low-draw decks.
- Corrupt Heart: Beat of Death chips per card played; per-turn damage cap — sustained plans beat one-turn gimmicks.
- Bronze Automaton: Hyper Beam is telegraphed — use intent, not memorized turn counts.

Original synthesis also drew on slaythespire.gg strategy guide cross-checked to Fandom for mechanics.
