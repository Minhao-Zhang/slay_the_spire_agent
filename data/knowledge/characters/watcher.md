# Watcher picks

- **Stances**: never enter **Wrath** without a kill, block, or exit (Calm, Wallop, Talk to the Hand, etc.).
- **Scrying**: set up wrath turns and avoid drawing dead on critical turns.
- **Calm / Divinity**: treat energy spikes as burst windows—don’t waste on low-impact plays.
- **Removal heuristics**: **Defends before Strikes**—**Wrath** doubles attack damage (Strikes scale) but not block; Defends clog burst-kill turns. Purge multiple Defends before Strike removals once stance damage is online; still keep enough block to survive double-taken damage.
- Rewards: favor safe stance transitions and reliable block payoffs.
