# Ironclad picks

- **Burning Blood** (starter relic): heals after each combat—often correct to **trade HP** for faster kills when you won’t die on the next swing.
- **Strength / exhaust**: convert strength into fast kills; Barricade lines need real block engines—don’t dilute with low-tempo filler.
- **Corruption + Dead Branch**: high variance—take with draw, energy, or exhaust payoffs.
- **Removal heuristics**: **Strikes** usually first—late Strikes are weak once scaling attacks exist; **Defends** stay useful as cheap fuel for exhaust (True Grit, Second Wind, Corruption lines). Early **Perfected Strike** can justify keeping Strikes longer.
- Rewards: prioritize cards that solve Nob/Lagavulin early, then scaling for longer fights.
