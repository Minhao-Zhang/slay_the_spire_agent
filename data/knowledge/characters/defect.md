# Defect picks

- **Orbs**: plan channel, focus, evoke—don’t float orbs with no payoff line. Full slots **evoke** the rightmost when you channel another.
- **Claw**: needs thin deck or heavy draw—verify multiple cheap plays per turn.
- **Frost / block**: great for long fights; pair with real scaling damage to avoid timer losses.
- **Removal heuristics**: **Strikes** once **orbs** carry damage—basic Strikes become dead draws; **Defends** matter early until **Frost** block is reliable.
- Prioritize stabilizing Act 1 elites while keeping a late win condition.
