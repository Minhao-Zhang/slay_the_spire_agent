# Silent picks

- **Poison / scaling**: needs time + defense—Weak and efficient block multiply poison lines.
- **Shivs**: need Accuracy, draw, or damage scaling—don’t take thin shiv packages without enablers.
- **Discard**: needs energy and outlets—skip payoffs without churn you can afford.
- **Removal heuristics**: **Strikes** aggressively—starter deck skews defensive (extra Defend + Survivor); you need efficient damage for Act 1 elites. **Footwork** keeps basic Defends relevant longer; remove curses and dead draws before cutting real block.
- Early Act 1: efficient damage + block often beat slow poison starts unless defense is already solved.
