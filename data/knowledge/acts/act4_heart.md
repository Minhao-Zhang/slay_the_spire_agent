# Act 4 and Corrupt Heart

- **Keys** (macro cost): **Ruby**—use campfire **Recall** (lose that site’s rest/smith); **Sapphire**—take key from chest **instead of** the relic; **Emerald**—beat a **flaming elite** (tougher elite). Often delay sapphire/emerald until the deck can absorb the cost.
- Need **consistent block + scaling damage** for Act 4 elites and Heart.
- **Beat of Death**: chip when you **play a card**—punishes tiny plays unless you have per-play block (After Image, Tungsten Rod–style mitigation, etc.) or similar; strength reduction still helps incoming hits.
- Heart **shoves statuses** into the deck early—plan **draw**, **exhaust**, Medical Kit–style outs, or saved **utility potions** to survive turn-2 spikes.
- **Per-turn damage cap** on the Heart (floor varies by ascension)—plan **multi-turn** sustained damage + defense, not only one-turn infinite fantasies.
- **Sozu** (+Energy, no potions) is painful here; value potion slots if Act 4 is the goal.
- Confirm numbers from **in-game** UI.
