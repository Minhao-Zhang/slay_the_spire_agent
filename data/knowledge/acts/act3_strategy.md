# Act 3 strategy

- Prefer **reliable** turns over lines that need perfect draw order. Bank potions and spike tools for elites and the boss path.
- Hall fights are punishing (exploders, spikers, Darklings, Transient, etc.)—fix deck holes at shop/rest/event **before** greedy paths.
- **Act 3 elites and bosses** (Reptomancer, Nemesis, Giant Head, Awakened One, Donu & Deca, Time Eater): detailed notes in `combat/elite_strategies.md` and `combat/boss_strategies.md` (daggers, Intangible windows, Slow, Power timing, focus order, 12-card rule).
- Path to boss: favor **rest** if sustain cannot survive two bad draws in a row.
