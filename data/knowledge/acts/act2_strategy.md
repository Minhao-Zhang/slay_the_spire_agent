# Act 2 strategy

- Enemy scaling jumps—**AoE** and **front-loaded block** matter. Multi-enemy rooms need a clear kill order (disruptors and high-damage enemies first).
- If hallway damage is weak, you need **scaling** (Strength, poison/catalyst, X-cost, relic-driven engines) before the act boss.
- Hallway shape checks: Byrds (multi-hit to ground), Snake Plant (many small hits punished), Shelled Parasite (stall punished), triple Cultist (AoE or big single hits).
- Events are spikier than Act 1—avoid high-variance choices on low HP.
- **Act 2 elites and bosses** (Book of Stabbing, Gremlin Leader, Slavers, Champ, Collector, Bronze Automaton): use `combat/elite_strategies.md` and `combat/boss_strategies.md` for target priority and phase gates.
