# Act 1 strategy

- Act 1 is a **front-loaded damage race**: starter Strikes/Defends barely clear elites—draft premium attacks and early upgrades before slow engines.
- Front-load block only when it buys a turn you need; favor cards that answer the **next** gear check on your path.
- **Act 1 elites** (Gremlin Nob, Lagavulin, Three Sentinels): one-line tactics live in `combat/elite_strategies.md`—read that for Nob/Skill interaction, sleep setup, and Dazed flood.
- **Act 1 bosses**: Hexaghost (Divider vs current HP—read intent); Slime Boss (race pre-split, AoE for splits); Guardian (thorns defensive phase—time damage).
- Pathing: elites snowball relics/gold when HP and potions allow; avoid chained elites with no rest/shop off-ramp if the deck is thin.
