# Potion economy

- Potions are **stored tempo**: using one to prevent 20+ HP loss now is usually better than holding it for a hypothetical later crisis.
- **Categories**: **aggressive** (Fire, Attack, Strength) shorten fights—often turn 1 on Nob-style races; **defensive** (Block, Ghost, weak cleanse) answer telegraphed hits; **utility** (draw, energy, heal) fix a bad hand or stabilize; **scaling** (Cultist, Power-style) front-load engine setup in long Act 3 / boss fights.
- Spend offensive potions early in elites/bosses when they flip lethal or save large HP; spend defensive potions when a telegraphed large hit would otherwise connect; don’t die with full slots.
- If slots are full and a potion reward is coming, **drink one first** so the new potion isn’t wasted.
- Don’t enter a boss with all slots full—use at least one on the path or in the fight opener.
- Treat potions as part of your elite/boss **gear check**: a potion spent to secure a relic or skip a rest is often ROI-positive.
