# Elite combat reference

- Elites are **gear checks**: have a plan for turns 1–3. A potion that saves 30+ HP often pays for a future elite.
- Respect **intent**—if a big hit is telegraphed, blocking can beat greedy damage if you would die next turn.
- **Act 1 — Gremlin Nob**: Skills feed Rage/Strength—**damage race**; favor Attacks and burst; excessive Defends accelerate enemy damage.
- **Act 1 — Lagavulin**: Free setup while asleep (Powers, draw); after wake, kill before **−Str/−Dex** makes your deck useless.
- **Act 1 — Three Sentinels**: Kill an outer Sentinel fast; they **shuffle Dazed** into your deck—slow clears mean hands full of blanks. AoE helps; track attack pattern.
- **Act 1 — Slavers**: Prioritize the controller (e.g. net/Entangle) before cleanup.
- **Act 2 — Book of Stabbing**: End fast; **Wounds** choke the deck; Weak and Strength reduction help vs multi-hit.
- **Act 2 — Gremlin Leader**: Burn the boss or clear adds with AoE—do not let buff cycles run.
- **Act 2 — Bronze Automaton**: Plan **Hyper Beam** from intent—race or concrete block/intangible; orbs can return stolen cards when killed.
- **Act 3 — Reptomancer**: Kill **daggers** before they detonate; AoE is high value.
- **Act 3 — Nemesis**: Use **Intangible** turns to set up; strike on vulnerable windows.
- **Act 3 — Giant Head**: **Slow** rewards playing low-impact setup before big attacks.
