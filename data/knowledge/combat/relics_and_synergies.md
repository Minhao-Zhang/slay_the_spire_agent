# Relic ecosystem

- Boss and elite relics often **define win conditions**—build around what you’re offered; pivot picks if a relic enables an engine (e.g. energy, draw, exhaust).
- **Boss-relic deck shifts**: **Snecko Eye** (+draw, confused costs)—bias draft toward **higher-cost payoffs**, fewer fragile 0-cost engines; **Runic Pyramid** (no hand discard)—add **exhaust**, draw, or energy to avoid curse/status **hand clog**.
- **Pathing enablers**: out-of-campfire healing (e.g. Meat on Bone, Pantograph) and **campfire extras** (Shovel, Peace Pipe, Girya) change which routes are profitable.
- **Sustain relics** (regen, max HP, healing) raise your risk budget for elite routes.
- **Front-loaded relics** (damage, free damage skills) solve Act 1 checks; **scaling relics** pay off in Act 2–3.
- Avoid taking card rewards that ignore what relics already solve—reduce redundancy, increase synergy density.
- Shop relics: buy what fixes the next check, not the shiniest rare if the deck still dies to the next elite.
