# Boss combat reference

- Identify the **loss clock** (scaling buffs, multi-hit, strength gain, minion waves) and spend mitigation and damage there.
- Do not over-kill adds if the **boss phase** or timer is the real threat.
- Front-load block on telegraphed spikes; chip damage is fine if you survive the big turn.
- **Act 1 — Hexaghost**: Needs scaling damage; **Divider** scales with **current HP** at resolution—read intent; entering at lower HP can reduce that hit.
- **Act 1 — Slime Boss**: Race main HP pre-split; **AoE** for slime adds; Evolve-style tools help vs Slimed.
- **Act 1 — Guardian**: Alternating modes—**thorns while defensive**; time damage for safe windows, keep block when thorns are up.
- **Act 2 — Champ**: Do not cross **50% HP** until ready—phase 2 hits much harder.
- **Act 2 — Collector**: Minion pressure—don’t stall; AoE + burst; sometimes leave one weak minion to disrupt resummon rhythm.
- **Act 2 — Bronze Automaton**: Same as elite: **Hyper Beam** from intent, orb tactics.
- **Act 3 — Awakened One**: Clear cultists first; delay non-essential **Powers** while passive gains Str from Powers; unload after phase change.
- **Act 3 — Donu & Deca**: If you cannot out-block Donu’s scaling, focus **Donu**; if **Dazes** choke a low-draw deck, focus **Deca**. Watch **Artifact**.
- **Act 3 — Time Eater**: **12 cards played** ends your turn early and it gains Strength—spread value; avoid ending at 10–11 plays without a plan; handle heal-to-half if you cannot burst.
